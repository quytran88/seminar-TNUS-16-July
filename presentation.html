Reproducible Research

Ni dung nghin cu kh lp

“Nghin cu khoa hc kh lp” - reproducible research ang l xu th mi ca gii khoa hc v nhng package s gip ngi nghin cu khoa hc hon ton c th vit bo co cho ring mnh.“Nghin cu khoa hc kh lp” l loi nghin cu khoa hc khng ch c bo co kt qu nghin cu m cn bao gm y cc bc thc hin, ng thi cng b cc thng tin cn thit (d liu, thut ton s dng,) ngi khc c th tin hnh li nghin cu nhm xc nhn tnh ng n v m rng nghin cu da trn nn tng nghin cu c. y “reproducible” hay “reproducibility” mang ngha c kh nng lp li, c kh nng thc hin li nghin cu c cng b.

S dng Rmarkdown v knitr l s la chn ti u cho xu hng “reproducible research” ny. Cc bc tun t (sequence) a ra kt qu da trn YAML metadata, code chunks, text nhm a ra mt qu trnh t u t chy code, hin th d liu, show kt qu v cui cng l report di nhiu dng nh beamer_presentation, ioslides_presentation, revealjs::revealjs_presentation

Quy trnh ca Rmarkdown kh n gin nhng li cung cp mt quy trnh kh y gip ngi nghin cu c th cm nhn tng bc xy dng, gii quyt v c th ha a ra kt qu ca bi ton. T c th nghin cu, pht trin su hn cc ni dung nghin cu tip theo.
